Shipping & Delivery Policy

Freshness guaranteed. Learn how we deliver our premium catfish to your door.

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to all major cities across Ghana, including:

  • Accra - All suburbs including Ablekuma, Dansoman, Spintex, East Legon, and more
  • Tema - Community 1-25, Ashaiman, and surrounding areas
  • Kumasi - Central business district and surrounding suburbs
  • Takoradi - Sekondi and surrounding areas
  • Cape Coast - City center and nearby towns

For locations outside these areas, please contact us to discuss delivery options.

Delivery Times

Standard Delivery: 1-3 business days within Accra, 2-5 business days for other cities.

Express Delivery: Same-day delivery for orders placed before 10am within Accra (additional fee applies).

Bulk Orders: Scheduled delivery based on your preferred frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly).

Freshness Guarantee: All deliveries are made using refrigerated vehicles to ensure your catfish arrives fresh and at optimal temperature.

Delivery Fees

  • Within Accra: GHS 30 - GHS 50 depending on distance
  • Tema: GHS 40 - GHS 60
  • Kumasi / Takoradi / Cape Coast: GHS 80 - GHS 120
  • Bulk Orders (100kg+): Free delivery within Accra and Tema

📦 Special Note: Delivery fees are subject to change based on fuel prices and distance. You will be informed of the exact fee when you place your order.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 24 hours of confirmation. For bulk orders, we require at least 48-72 hours notice to prepare your requested quantity.

Once your order is ready, you will receive:

  • 📧 Email confirmation with delivery details
  • 📱 SMS notification on the day of delivery
  • 📞 Phone call from our delivery driver 30 minutes before arrival

Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not satisfied with the quality of your catfish:

  • Within 2 hours of delivery: Contact us for a replacement or refund
  • Quality issues: Photos required for verification
  • Wrong items delivered: We will redeliver the correct items at no cost

For any delivery-related concerns, please email ediblefarms000@gmail.com or call +233 54 660 0063.

Freshness Guarantee

We take pride in delivering the freshest catfish possible. Our process ensures:

  • 🐟 Harvested within 12 hours of delivery
  • ❄️ Kept refrigerated throughout transit
  • 📦 Packed in insulated, leak-proof packaging
  • 🌡️ Temperature-checked before dispatch
If your fish doesn't meet your expectations, we'll make it right. Promise.
